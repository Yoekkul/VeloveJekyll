Introductory Workshops

These workshops are teach you the anatomy of a bike and how to fix common problems. The workshop is open to anyone, no prior knwoledge or experience about bike anatomy or repair is required.
The duration of the workshop is about 1.5 hours and will cover:
  • Bike anatomy: What is all this stuff? What is it called? What does it do?
  • Common problems and fixes: brake and gear shifting problems etc.
  • Hands on practice fixing a flat tyre.

Complete the Registration Form to see the dates for upcoming workshops and to register.

Social Rides

We regulalry organize short guided tours (about 1 hour) in the Zürich area. Complete the Registration Form to see the dates and details for upcoming social rides, and register for the rides that suit your riding style (or maybe try out something new).

In addition last friday of every month there is a Critical Mass that happens. This Wikipedia page explains the Critical Mass concept, in short, it is a ride that celebrates how awesome bikes are, and also acts as a form of social movement by cyclists reclaiming the streets. This facebook page provides more details about the Critical Mass events in Zürich.
