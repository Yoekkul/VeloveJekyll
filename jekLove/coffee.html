Irrational coffee

Irrational Co. Irrational Coffee is an AMIV commission dedicated to spreading alternative coffee culture.
If you are interested in learning more about different coffee beans, processing techniques, brewing methods, or anything coffee-related, then this is the place for you!

You can find our stand on Thursdays 12:00-14:00 in the CAB courtyard where you can taste different coffees and cascara tea.
If Espresso is more your thing visit us on Tuesdays 12:00-14:00 in the VELOVE Velowerkstatt on Hönggerberg.
Irrational Coffee also organizes coffee workshops and other coffee-related events, such as trips and coffee «pub crawls».
