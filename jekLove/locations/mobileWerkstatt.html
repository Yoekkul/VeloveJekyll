Mobile Bike Workshop

With our mobile workshop we will be able to come to your campus.
We will help students and campus staff in fixing their bikes, providing tools and knowledge to get their bikes road worthy again.

We, the VELOVE team, are a group of bike enthusiast students, and our goal is to get more students exited about biking and create a space for encounters all around biking.
